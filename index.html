Frequently asked questions

How long does this process take?

Many clients see movement in 30–90 days, but timing depends on the bureaus, furnishers, and your unique situation.

Do I need all three credit reports?

Yes. We need Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion to see the full picture. Please pull all three reports before your consultation so we can dive straight into strategy instead of waiting on logins.

Can you guarantee deletions or score increases?

No one can legally guarantee results. We challenge inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable items and coach you on healthy credit behavior.

Is this legal?

Yes. Credit repair is legal when done under the rules of the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) and the Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA). We do not create new identities or CPNs.
