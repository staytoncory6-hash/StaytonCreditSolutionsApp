Frequently asked questions

How long does credit repair take?

Most see movement within 30–90 days, but timing varies by case and response from bureaus/furnishers.

Can you guarantee deletions?

No one can legally guarantee results. We dispute inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable items under the FCRA and escalate with proof.

Do I need all three bureau reports?

Yes—errors can appear on one but not another. We pull or help you obtain all three.

What’s the best way to raise my score?

Manage utilization, build positive history, fix errors, and keep on‑time payments. We provide a customized plan.
