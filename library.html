Checklists

Identity Theft Remediation

  • Complete FTC Identity Theft Report
  • File a police report (keep copies)
  • Place fraud alerts and consider credit freezes
  • Contact creditors/collectors; dispute fraudulent items
  • Include ID and proof of address with disputes
  • Track confirmations and follow-up dates

Bureau Dispute Packet

  • Cover letter (who/what/why), dispute reasons, and requested correction
  • Driver’s license (or ID) and proof of address
  • Account statements, screenshots, payment receipts
  • Mail by certified mail; keep receipts and copies

Utilization Strategy (Score Lift)

  • Target overall utilization under 30% (10% is ideal)
  • Distribute balances to avoid any one card > 30%
  • Keep oldest positive trade lines open
  • Set autopay to avoid missed payments

Evidence Pack (What to Include)

Core Documents

  • Photo ID, proof of address (utility bill, lease, bank statement)
  • Credit report pages highlighting the error(s)
  • Payment proof, settlement letters, creditor emails
  • Police report / FTC report (for identity theft)

Timeline & Tracking

  • Send disputes via certified mail; save tracking
  • Note bureau’s 30-day response window
  • Record investigation results and next steps

Compliance Notes

  • No one can legally guarantee deletions or score changes (CROA)
  • Dispute only inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable items
  • Keep your file organized (PDF binder with dates)
